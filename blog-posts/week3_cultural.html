Title: How can your Gregorc Thinking style help you improve your learning at DBC? What has been the biggest struggle during DBC Phase 0? Is it related to your learning style? If so, how can you adapt?

Date:9/14/14

My Gregorc style is Abstract Sequential. This type of learner likes to analyze situations before making a decision and applies logic in solving problems. While I think these qualities may fit me, I don't know that I fit into this mold perfectly. Abstract sequential people have difficlty working in groups and repeating tasks. I think I can do good at both of these. So, I don't know that my Gregorc style helps that much. My approach to solving problems often deals with thinking and trial and error, but I also learn well by dealing with others.

My biggest struggle in phase 0 was due to having to start the program at a later point than others and completing multiple sections in a couple days. Though it was time-consuming, I was able to do it. I don't think this has to do with my learning style.
