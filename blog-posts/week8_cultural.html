Title: Dealing with Conflict

Date:

Discuss a time you had an emotional conflict with someone. What was it about? What happened?

I don't really have many emotional conflicts, so it is hard to think of one. To make one up, let's say my friend and I fought over some collectible cards when I was a child.

What was the source of the conflict?

My friend wanted the same card that I had.

How did you handle that conflict?

He yelled and screamed and said he deserved the card, but I had purchased it with the money my grandmother had given me for my birthday. To make him happy, I allowed him to borrow it for a while, and that resolved the issue.

Did your actions make the conflict better or worse?

My actions made the situation better.

If you could go back, what would you do differently, if anything?

I really don't think I even needed to let my friend borrow the card. What I did was nice, but I am not sure it was necessary.

What did you learn from this experience?

I learned that a little bit of kindness can go a long way.
